Key findings

From 2018 to 2020:

  • 533 cyclists and 708 pedestrians suffered casualties on Bradford’s roads
  • 74% of the pedestrians were more than 50 metres from a pedestrian crossing
  • 85% of pedestrian casualties involved a car
  • 44% of cyclist casualties involved a car
  • 91% of the cyclists were male
